QUESTION CARD !
Player gets a random pirate-related question.

MUTINY !
There is a mutiny on board the ship. For the entire next round the captain is countered with every card.

WATERFALL !

Everybody finishes their drink one at a time starting from the captain. Deducts points from random players that were near drowning. YIKES!

COUNTER CARD !
Player gets to counter a captain\'s card.

DRINK CARD !
Player rolls two dice. The crew member takes X sips depending on the outcome.
